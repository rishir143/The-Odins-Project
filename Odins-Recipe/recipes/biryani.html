Chicken Biryani

The Recipe of Chiken Biryani




Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron milk (optional)

Steps to follow to Cook

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, biryani masala, and salt for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  4. Add whole spices and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  6. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 10–15 minutes until partially cooked.
  7. In another pot, boil the soaked rice until it is about 70% cooked, then drain the water.
  8. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture.
  9. Sprinkle fried onions, chopped coriander, mint leaves, and saffron milk over the rice.
  10. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes.
  11. Gently mix the layers before serving.
  12. Serve hot with raita or salad.
