Creamy Alfredo Pasta

The Recipe of Creamy Alfredo Pasta




Ingredients

  • 250g fettuccine or penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh parsley
  • Grilled chicken or mushrooms (optional)

Steps to follow to Cook

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Reserve half a cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  4. Melt butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  5. Add minced garlic and sauté for about one minute.
  6. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer gently.
  7. Add the Parmesan cheese gradually while stirring continuously.
  8. Season with salt and black pepper.
  9. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until evenly coated.
  10. If the sauce becomes too thick, add a little reserved pasta water.
